Profile

Engineering direction

I am an aerospace engineering graduate focused on propulsion systems, spacecraft hardware, thermal-fluid analysis, and computational engineering. I like work where the physics, code, CAD, instrumentation, and final technical story all need to agree.

My academic project base includes cold-gas and resistojet thruster research, shock-tube pressure processing, SR-30 gas turbine analysis, wind-tunnel coefficient extraction, modal wing analysis, and turbofan cycle modeling. My newer Vinnotek work adds copper-AM cold-plate analysis, additive manufacturing screening, internal AI automation, and practical technical documentation.

What I bring

  • Derive the model, write the code, and check the assumptions.
  • Turn imperfect experimental data into defensible engineering decisions.
  • Bridge aerospace analysis, CAD, AM process constraints, and test planning.
  • Use AI tools as engineering accelerators, with traceability and human review.
